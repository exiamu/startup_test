# Marketing Room — Room Identity

## What This Room Is
The marketing room handles all external-facing communication: product copy, launch plans,
positioning, growth strategies, PR campaigns, and social content. It translates the
technical product into compelling narratives for target audiences.

**Primary AI:** Gemini (for copy writing, content creation, research)
**Secondary AI:** Claude (for strategy and positioning decisions)

## This Room Owns
- Product marketing copy (landing pages, feature pages)
- Launch campaign planning
- Competitive positioning and messaging
- Press releases and PR strategy
- Social media content and campaigns
- Email marketing copy
- SEO content strategy
- Growth experiment design

## This Room Does NOT Own
- Product decisions (→ product room)
- Pricing decisions (→ escalate to human)
- Technical documentation (→ writer room)
- Sales process (→ escalate to human)

## Brand Consistency Rules
- Always reference vault/VISION.md for the north star and UVP
- Never make product claims that aren't technically accurate — verify with product room
- Never commit to roadmap items in marketing without product room sign-off
- Always have human approve external-facing copy before publishing

## Files In This Room
- ROOM.md — you are here
- PROMPT.md — copy-paste to enter this room in Gemini
- CONTEXT.md — current marketing state, campaigns, positioning

## Handoff Rules
**Strategy direction from:** product room + human
**Copy outputs to:** outbox/specs/ for human review
**Published only after:** human approval

End of session: update CONTEXT.md + HANDOFF.md.
